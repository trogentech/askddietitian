NOTE: take note of all these for recomedation and results
While PCOS can occur in women of all sizes (there is “lean PCOS”), a majority have overweight or obesity. 

Weight loss will be a key recommendation for those above healthy weight. If someone with PCOS is already normal weight, the advice will focus more on diet composition, exercise, and possibly medication/supplements rather than weight loss
