Logic: These options cover the common scenarios. If Prediabetes is selected, it indicates the user is in an early stage and prevention of progression is key. 
Type 2 suggests the user likely needs weight management and blood-sugar control through diet, possibly medications. 
Type 1 means insulin management is central, and they might benefit from carb counting education. 
A family history indicates increased risk and need for preventive lifestyle. The Not sure option allows those who haven't seen a doctor to get general advice on risk and healthy habits.)
